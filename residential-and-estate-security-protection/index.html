Residential and Estate Security and Protection

Specialized services for high-profile individuals and executives

We understand the dynamics and flexibility involved in having to manage a household actively engaged in enterprise; or a home with VIPs and their families. The logistics of keeping the residents’ personal life safe from outside threats when there are so many people coming and going can be unsustainable without professional help.

At Eagle Eye International Security Services, we implement a methodical approach and a procedural point of view when it comes to protecting you, your family, and your home. Our rigorous and thorough standards are a result of our many years of experience in the security sector. Our personnel have been trained by the finest law enforcement and military organizations in the world. Not only do they bring a depth of knowledge, they have a wealth of experience in seeing both distinct vulnerabilities and strategic successes when it comes to residential security.

Maintain the Safety and Security of your Home and Property

Having a layered approach to your home or estate’s security is going to ensure that gaps are filled and contingencies are in place. Our main goal is to keep the threats well outside, but we also fortify within the property boundaries to ensure the highest level of protection. Having a team of our qualified security agents on site will give you an incomparable sense of security and safety.

Why Choose Eagle Eye's Security Agents?

  • Active shooter training
  • Medical training
  • Defensive and evasive driver trained
  • Advanced weapons and tactics
  • And more depending on the scope of each assignment

Residential Home Security Agent Qualifications

We are dedicated to making sure you are comfortable with your team of agents who will be in your home and on your grounds. We want to make your experience with our agents as seamless, unobtrusive, and trouble-free as possible. We also know that having a security team that is flexible, adaptive, and courteous is going to make the whole operation run more smoothly for you and the agents.

Our agents go through a rigorous program of background checks to make sure that the ideal team of agents will be protecting your home. We develop a profile for each of our team members based on the Harrison Assessment Test, which can quantify, on paper, the traits we want in a resident or estate security team member.

Once we find the right candidate, they will undergo an extensive training program. Our training curriculum is a key reason for our reputation for success in the security industry.

Rigorously trained personnel by top law enforcement and military organizations

We firmly believe that the quality of the personnel that we hire will be the determining factor in the longevity, reliability, and success of all of our residential security details. You can rely on our men and women who are standing watch, they will be focused on a mindset built on observation, reporting, verification, and RESPONDING!

Our team of security professionals are also available if you or a family member needs to travel domestically or abroad securely.

You can trust in us. You can trust us with your home, your family, and your personal safety. Call us now and rest easier with Eagle Eye International Security as your security team.

GET IN TOUCH

Observe. Report. Respond.

When you choose Eagle Eye for your residential and estate security needs, you’ll soon learn that we take the job very seriously. You’ll find us actively and constantly observing your estate and the surrounding landscape. Our agents are always on the lookout for any signs of intrusions or threats to your person or property. We are devoted to the idea that vigilance will prevent most problems, but, should we discover any threats or breeches, we will take swift action to deal with them. At the end of the shift, our team will report any and all activity for the next shift of agents. Constantly open lines of communication between the team members and the client is how we keep homes and families safe.

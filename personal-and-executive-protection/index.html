Personal & Executive Protection

Executive protection is a highly specialized field of security that applies psychological, logistical, and physical components to protect corporate executives and their families. Providing top flight security for VIPs requires highly skilled and well-trained individuals with all of the necessary components to keep our clients safe. Eagle Eye's rigorous training includes developing acute tactical awareness, implementing interpersonal communication effectively, and improvisational problem-solving. Overall, our goal is to build a bedrock of knowledge that provides our agents with a foundation to use unique security solutions for protecting executives from a variety of potential threats.

Protective Services Based on Core Values

Our approach to this important work is built around our core values: integrity, ethics, trustworthiness, and accountability. The combination of these values, along with our years of significant in-the-field experience, means our executive protection personnel have a keen sense of reliability and safety in your service. Our professionally trained executive protection employees understand that having the utmost discretion, while possessing strong deductive reasoning skills is critical to success. We will ensure that you receive the highest value of service and feel a unique level of trust using our top quality executive protection services.

International Security Experience

For almost two decades, Eagle Eye International has been providing personal and executive protection services across the globe. We got our start in Mexico and California, and over those years we have slowly expanded our way into the far corners of the United States and the world. Currently Eagle Eye holds licenses in ten key states and are able to support our protective services in all 50 states. We are licensed to work on long-term projects in the following states or districts: Texas, New York, Florida, California, Arizona, Colorado, Hawaii, Rhode Island, Montana, Washington DC.

Working in and with Latin American countries has always been a strength for us, we provide top level security into all of the important commercial cities on the United States and Mexico border.

We are a Trusted International Executive Security Service

One of our key standouts is the amount of time and effort we place on core values and developing a culture of trust. We take our time in bringing in new hires, getting to know them, and always assigning to them an experienced mentor. These seasoned veterans will guide our incoming team members by instilling in them our dedication to high standards and proven protocols. Yes, it takes some time and effort, but we attribute our legacy of success to our calculated and methodical processes. Most of our agents and managerial staff have transitioned to security from distinguished military and law enforcement backgrounds. Our agents speak multiple languages, are well traveled, and are trained to provide protection.

Planning for Surprises

The executive protection services we offer can be as simple as a day trip into Mexico for an important business meeting; or consistent travel to and from areas where threats are a possibility whether in the United States or abroad. We provide prestigious concierge service to everything we do, including access to armored vehicles when necessary.

The key to a successful bodyguard or executive protection security detail is built around tight and thorough planning from the very beginning. Sloppy planning and a lax attitude about security can lead to dangerous situations. We have spent our careers in the military and law enforcement in preparing strategies, executing plans, and anticipating changes.

Our entire executive protection and bodyguard services is built around professionalism, discretion, and intuition. If you want to feel safe from the variety of threats out there, then give us a call for more information on our executive security services.

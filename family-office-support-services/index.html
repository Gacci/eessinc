Family Office Security & Protection Services

As a Family Office Manager, you're responsible for almost every facet of your client’s life—both professional and personal. It's an enormous responsibility and a complex undertaking. Part of your job is ensuring the security and welfare of your home office workers and possibly family members. Your security needs might include personal protection for traveling VIPs, physical presence and patrols on the property, access control, setting up and maintaining surveillance technology, and dealing with cybersecurity issues.

Eagle Eye International Protective Services wants you to know that when it comes to your client’s protection, we're the company to call first. After you do your research and take a look at the other security companies out there, you'll find that our family office security services will cover all of your security-related needs, ensuring the safety of the people and property under your charge.

Your client is likely high-profile: a lawyer, an investor, a C-suite executive, or a well-known entrepreneur. We work with these highly valuable individuals every day. We understand that there are a wide variety of special concerns that come with being a high-profile individual. And, we know that every one of these VIPs has different preferences and idiosyncrasies when it comes to their protection. As their family office manager, you're responsible for making sure they receive the best possible protective services that fit their particular set of circumstances.
